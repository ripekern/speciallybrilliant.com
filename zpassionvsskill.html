Passion vs Skill

There is no passion to be found playing small--in settling for a life that is less than the one you are capable of living. [Nelson Mandela]

When we are born into this world we are endowed with certain traits that give us special capabilities that can be either ignored or grown to fruition. These are called passions. We may think we learn these but those are actually called skills. It is very important to know these passions and the difference between skills, it is also extremely relevant to our success to our private and business relationships and passion can be sometimes hard to express when our life has not been nurtured and fed in a way that produces the fruit necessary. In this article I will go in detail about these essential facts.

If you do what you love, youâ€™ll never work a day in your life. [Marc Anthony]

Is work something worth doing or is it worthless? Some people feel as though their jobs are only meant for getting one thing and that is a certain amount of dollars per hour. They go as far as trying to make every hour make more dollars but never cross any boundaries or make any risks to think outside the box that they have been conditioned to enjoy. The importance of what we are passionate for is quickly diminished and hid away in this world we live. That cannot change the fact that the passion we were created with is not something we can get rid of. Deep down inside of our spirits we will always want what we desire. If we do not do what we are passionate for then we will turn to addictions and unpleasant personalities which are despised by everyone and further leading away from gaining any hope of having what we are passionate for. It is vital for our joy, mental and physical health to always pursue our passions and not settle for less. We could build skills in whatever job we get but that does not make it the right place to be. Also, it is vital to know our own passions by thinking deeply about it and analyzing our lives and what we enjoy. If we do not make the effort to do this work we cannot move forward with finding our greatest joys. In everything we do it is relevant and necessary because it will build a stronger community that is following our design and purpose.

Pleasure in the job puts perfection in the work. [Aristotle]

Have you ever felt burnt out or out of gas when it came to work? Has it ever felt like you hit a brick wall looking forever to the right and left while just feeling like there is no way around? This is a common and relevant feeling in most people on this earth. It is definitely hard to connect and release this feeling of hopelessness because we are not in a perfect world. At the same time it is deeply relevant to find a way around this wall, learn how to not be burnt out, and keep the gas in our tank. We need to focus on our passion and if we are not following our passion we need to relax and realize this truth. This may require us to come to a deep realization of its relevancy to us and that it's extremely vital to us. We may have been told that our passions are not relevant at all and we just need to focus instead on feeding the family or focusing on only doing what we are told. Basically this is the Babylonian system enslaving us from our joy and imprisoning us from this passion. The truth is that we are more successful and more productive when we are passionate about what we do. We cannot be made to be passionate about something but instead we need to discover it within ourselves. Once we realize this and make passion relevant in our lives we can pursue it because it becomes our priority. Our business and private relationships start to boom and we become healed from our wounds. Everyday we had not been pursuing our passion in life was a day we were being damaged in our spirit. This is why it is hard to express our passions after all the wounds have endured.

There will be day when everyone will know the truth and all will be able to pursue their passions. Right now it is rocky and people have a hard time trusting others. We need to be extra diligent to nurture our relationships and soften our tone and language with communicating. This is because it is hard to express our passions with all the wounds everyone has endured from loosing trust in people that are not as honest as they would like. A great way to express ourselves is to journal and to reflect upon the Word of God and share it. If we are to come to any sort of fruitfulness in our lives we need to know if what we are doing is right. If we consistently hurting others we are not going to be able to pursue any passion. When we open our hearts up to everyone and abandon the pain and suffering we have been holding onto then we can finally push through the wall we are facing. We simply have to let go of the lie that we are facing a wall and know that we can get through it because with God anything is possible.

With God we know that we can break through to new grounds. We can pursue our passions and we begin to realize the truth. We find the importance of our purpose on this earth and this drives us to let go of the chains we have been holding onto. We find that is is very relevant to every aspect of our lives and this further leads us to implement it and engrave it into our foundation. We finally are able to bring our past troubles and experiences to God who created the heaven, earth and sea and He helps us work through our damages and walls by making us into loving men and women. Overall we find that our passions are vital to our health and tranquility which brings us to peace. This is a constant journey that I have been on and I am glad to share more in the future.

Now the Lord of peace himself give you peace always by all means. The Lord be with you all. [ 2 Thessalonians 3:16 ]
